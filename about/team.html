Dr. Bin Hoang - Orange County Dentist

Dr. Hoang attended the University of California at San Diego with a Bachelor Degree in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology in 1998. He earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree at the University of Maryland Dental School in 2003. Dr. Hoang was part of the Millenium Dental Technologies team as an instructor providing a minimally invasive procedure called LANAP to patients suffering from periodontal disease.

Modern dentistry has improved in leaps and bounds with better advancements of dental technologies and clinical protocols that provides not only better, but more minimally invasive dentistry. Dr. Hoang has dedicated thousands of hours of continuing education to not only stay up to date but to be more ahead of the game to provide the best and minimally invasive dentistry for his patients, treating them with compassion like they are his own family member.

Dr. Hoang received his Fellowship status at the American Academy of Implant Dentistry. Using the modern advances of 3D conebeam technology and digital CAD/CAM Cerec technology, he is able to provide accurate and safe 3D guided implant surgeries.

He received an Orthodontic training and certification at the United States Dental Institute in 2005 and also with Invisalign, Clear Correct and MTM certifications. He understands that the majority of patients who have a fear of going to the dentist has a their own personal horror story. For this reason, he got training in Oral Conscious Sedation (twilight sleep dentistry) which provides a safe, and effective way to overcome those fears and provide comfortable dentistry.

Dr. Hoang is married to Mai with three beautiful daughters named Mia, Maily and Ami. Mia (7 yrs old) is currently training as a white belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and just won her first gold medal in the LA Jiu Jitsu tournament at Cerritos College June of 2015. Maily (6) loves gymnastics. Ami (4) likes to pick on her older sisters. On his spare time, Dr. Hoang enjoys family life, taking his family to Lakers and Angels games, and go to Disneyland. To stay in good physical shape, he plays volleyball with friends.
